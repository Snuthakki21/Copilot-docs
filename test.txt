CONTEXT YOU NEED

The existing build uses the business checking open primary indicator

A business checking account can be tied to two ECNs: a business ECN (the
entity, often with no email on file because Wells Fargo doesn't require
email at business signup) and a consumer ECN (the human owner, who usually
does have an email). The current hypothesis is that the audience is passing
the business ECN paired with the consumer's email to the consent engine,
and the consent engine is rejecting that pairing as invalid — which would
explain why ~30% of the audience is being dropped at the email scrub even
though the emails themselves don't look bad.
